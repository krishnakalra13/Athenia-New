Euphoria The jingles of joy

**Welcome to Euphoria 2024: उल् ला स-ए-महो त् सव,** a celebration of talent, creativity, and competition hosted by Athenia High School, Saharanpur! Set to take place on **Wednesday, 9th October 2024,** this inter-school competition invites students to push their limits and express their best skills in an array of unique and challenging events. The festival is crafted to nurture diverse talents from various age groups, starting with **Kindergarten** all the way to **Grade 12.** Each event is meticulously designed to offer students a stage where their potential can shine. Here’s a glimpse into what awaits:
1. **Kindergarten & Grade 1 - Mimic Magic:** A captivating non-verbal storytelling contest, focusing on the theme of fables. Young participants will use expressive gestures and costumes to convey their tales without words.
2. **Grades 2 & 3 - The Yoga Odyssey:** Students will showcase their yogic grace, moving seamlessly through various asanas that combine flexibility with artistry.
3. **Grades 4 & 5 - Sight and Sound Spectacular, Maths Checkup (Numeric Nexus), Waves of Grace, and का व्यां जलि:** From displaying analytical skills in math to expressing themselves through poetry and dance, this age group will experience a blend of intellectual and artistic events.
4. **Grades 6 to 8 - Comedy Chronicles (Hollywood Edition), समा चा र वा र्ता, Karaoke (Lyric Legends Live):** These middle-grade participants will take on fun challenges like performing a Hollywood-themed skit, news anchoring in Hindi, and belting out courage-themed karaoke.
5. **Grades 9 to 12 - The Eureka Challenge, Global Wonder Expedition, Stockamania, Human Canvas, Epic Edit Emporium:** Senior students will dive into intellectually stimulating contests like a science quiz, an exploration of world wonders, a stock market simulation, live sketching, and video editing.
Each event has specific guidelines to ensure fair play and creativity, as well as judgement criteria that focus on skills like accuracy, creativity, stage presence, and technique. Schools are encouraged to register by **8th September 2024**, bringing with them two accompanying teachers and an official confirmation of participants. The anticipation for Euphoria 2024 is electric, promising an enriching day where every student, regardless of winning or losing, will leave with memories and growth. This is more than a competition; it’s a platform where young minds meet, connect, and shine.
