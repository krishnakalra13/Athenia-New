About ATHENIA HIGH SCHOOL

MISSION:

SEEK WISDOM AND STRIVE FOR SOCIAL, ETHICAL AND EMOTIONAL LEARNING OF THE CHILD.


    Athenia High is synonymous with quality education and is a pioneer in mooting all-round development. It fosters an education system which aims at the flowering of the child's physical, emotional, mental and spiritual personality. Our endeavour is to impart education which looks beyond the frontiers of formal education. It is, in fact, a preparation for lifelong learning.

    We Aim At:


  • maintaining a global perspective
  • embracing a diversity of cultures represented by its staff and students

    Academics Success at Athenia is :


  • Acquisition of academic skills
  • A curriculum emphasising practical, technological approach of imparting
  • Taking up Languages as a skill development tool
  • Training to apply knowledge to real-life situations
  • Provision of qualified staff to ensure quality education
  • To facilitate holistic academic growth and development
  • Benchmarked results and learning outcomes on global standards

    Social Success at Athenia is :


  • development of a strong identity and self worth; ability to relate, connect and communicate effectively with others
  • cultural sensitivity and acceptance
  • valuing justice and fairness in social living
  • being service-oriented unconditionally
  • having an acceptable level of decorum
    Personal Success at Athenia is :
  • being a lifelong learner equipped with a keen spirit of inquiry
  • developing a value-based and ethical outlook on life
  • imbibing cognitive and behavioural skills to take on the world
  • Globally-minded Citizens at Athenia :
  • value the human spirit beyond the constraints of cultural boundaries
  • develop awareness and sensitivity to global issues encompassing all aspects of life
  • understand the impact of one’s thoughts and actions on the world at large
  • think globally and act locally





VISION:

“TEACH WITH PASSION – TRAIN WITH A VISION”
EACH ONE - TEACH ONE


    Our vision aligns with shifts in the global economy, society, and environment, which animates our mission and our values.We also believe in letting actions speak louder than words. We envision to be one of the leading schools in the community. We are widely acknowledged as a secular institution, which develops character and ethical leadership in its students.
    Athenia is a school which is built and run by thinkers who believe in secularism.
    It strives to nurture thinking students who reflect deeply on how they want to fashion a future world and have the resolve to see their ideas through.

    We believe excellence in learning and hard work must go hand-in-hand with humility and integrity. Knowledge acquired from education is not merely for the cultivation of our sensibilities or a step towards elevated employment but must be used in the service of humanity.
  • Students will be thinkers
  • Students will be communicators
  • Students will be principled
  • Students will be risk takers
  • Students will be balanced
  • Students will be reflective.
    In the near future, school will be more fluid, teachers more adaptable and flexible, students more knowledgable..

Committed to Excellence:

    The transformative learning experiences at Athenia High School is designed to help our students grow both in and out of the classroom. Established in 2017, Athenia High School is located in Saharanpur and reflects vibrant energy of the area. The school has lush green surroundings which help children to reflect on all areas of life. The school follows theory of Vasudhaiva Kutumbakam. Our passionate and skilled team members are here to help students make impact on the world. Students are ready to explore and reach greater heights.
