    Environment education. School believes in nature and it's conservation. Every strategy is made keeping in mind mother Earth. Biophilic design of school building, 100% electricity demand met through solar energy, 100% water harvesting are the best example of it. Students are always motivated to be in contact with nature, be it in the form of painting competitions, poster making competitions, special assemblies on save water, save earth, Mother nature, Pollution and associated hazards etc.
    School strongly propagates pollution free environment by being plastic free zone and motivating each student to be a part of plantation drive. Every student has formed a habit to plant a tree on their special occasions (e.g. Birthdays) in school or at their home. School actively participated in “BLENDED CAPACITY BUILDING PROGRAMME UNDER NAMAMI GANGA PROJECT “. School has also participated in paryavaran competition in which a team of 5 students with one teacher was required to study environmental conditions and how could they be helpful in the sustainable development. Athenia High has Eco club called “Tree People “. This club is responsible for plantation and upkeep of trees in the school premises.
